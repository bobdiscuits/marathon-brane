FACTION: TRAXUS OFFWORLD INDUSTRIES \[Traxus\]

ROLE: Interplanetary industrial and technological conglomerate

CLASSIFICATION: Megacorporation

AGENT: Vulcan

  

OVERVIEW: TRAXUS is the leading industrial, technology, and space mining megacorporation across Sol. Recognized as the most powerful private corporation in human history, TRAXUS's influence is second only to the UNIFIED EARTH SPACE COUNCIL \[UESC\].

  

TRAXUS played a key role in the MARATHON ship's development, construction, and subsequent mission to TAU CETI IV. As such, TRAXUS have publicly proclaimed their rights of reacquisition to all proprietary technology and associated developments tied to the MARATHON and the TAU CETI colony. They have not, however, made public their use of RUNNER operatives to scavenge the colony in direct opposition of UESC forces.
