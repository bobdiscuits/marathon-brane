    • CYBERACME SYSTEMS

DESIGN FUNCTION: Bio-cybernetic shell augmentation

  

OVERVIEW: CRADLES are key components in SHELL development. A CRADLE houses, nurtures, breeds, and focuses dedicated WEAVEWORM colonies programmed and assigned to its dedicated biomatic print processes. Engineered as part of SEKIGUCHI GENETICS patented SHELL "birthing" science, CRADLES are vital to the creation, customization, and upkeep of a healthy, operational SHELL.

  

Each CRADLE relies on CYBERACME sub-systems and neural support links to bolster the connectivity between the CRADLE's function, each worm cluster and individual worm, and each printed SHELL's central operations. This provides a RUNNER's ONI with direct access to not only the CRADLE's proprietary systems, but also to opportunities for unique jailbreaking of a CRADLE's functions. Any reprogramming voids all warranties, but few RUNNERS care about the legality of the tech they use for illegal purposes.

  

REMARK: The circumstances around RUNNER presence in the TAU CETI system give CYBERACME unique control over RUNNER access to CRADLES.

SHELL TECH: CRADLE FUNCTION

\===================================================================

  

CORE CRADLE FUNCTION: The WEAVEworm colonies housed within a CRADLE require energy to operate at peak efficiency. A RUNNER's ONI, as approved by CYBERACME, can provide that RUNNER's CRADLE with energy upon successful completion of CONTRACTS \[offer not limited to CYBERACME CONTRACT completion\]. But a RUNNER can also "feed" its CRADLE via the MATTER CONVERTER inside their VAULT.

  

The use of a MATTER CONVERTER to break down unwanted or excess goods \[weapons, materials, consumables, etc.\] provides raw matter as sustenance for WEAVEWORMS while in their dormant state between SHELL printing or augmentation. Keeping each colony fed maintains their operation integrity while also ensuring their longevity.

  

RUNNERS have a constant need for SHELL enhancement, repair, and replacement. Maintaining WEAVEWORM colony health and learning the value in how, when, and the cost of SHELL augmentation via the CRADLE can elevate a RUNNER's odds for survival, so be sure to maintain your CRADLE and nurse your colonies with care.

SHELL TECH: CRADLE CUSTOMIZATION

\===================================================================

  

WEAVEWORMS, THE CRADLE, AND YOU: To get the best out of the CRADLE, a RUNNER must familiarize themself with options available to improve a SHELL's operations and abilities.

  

A CRADLE typically houses six types of WEAVEWORM colonies, each programmed with a focus on a specific attribute within the SHELL's "living" bio-cybernetic systems. Those attributes are…

    • STRENGTH

    • RECHARGE

    • DEXTERITY

    • ENDURANCE

    • SUPPORT

    • RESISTANCE

  

A RUNNER feeds their WEAVEWORM colonies to increase their CRADLE's available energy, then selects available stats within their CRADLE's attributes to improve each over time.

  

It is up to the individual RUNNER how they choose to boost a given stat line. Preferred SHELLS or run-styles will inform CRADLE customization. There is no right or wrong path toward becoming the best version of your synthetic self.

  

REMARK: At any point, a RUNNER can choose to reset their CRADLE, allowing for trial and error as they test the potential of the SHELLS.

SHELL TECH: CRADLE OPTIMIZATION

\===================================================================

  

GETTING THE MOST FROM YOUR SHELLS: Each track of stats connected to a CRADLE's SHELL attributes improves dedicated functionalities with the SHELL's systems. Additionally, each track contains boosted thresholds, offering specific advantages within an attribute's focus. The breakdown below highlights the focus areas available to optimize your CRADLE for peak SHELL operation…

  

STRENGTH

    • FOCUS: Melee Damage, Finisher Siphon

RECHARGE

    • FOCUS: Prime Recovery, Tactical Recovery

DEXTERITY

    • FOCUS: Agility, Loot Speed

ENDURANCE

    • FOCUS: Heat Capacity, Fall Resistance

SUPPORT

    • FOCUS: Revive Speed, Ping Duration

RESISTANCE

    • FOCUS: Self-Repair Speed, Hardware, Firewall

  

Understanding how increased stats within and across attributes crafts SHELL function to fit a RUNNER's needs and comfort will allow that RUNNER to operate more efficiently, more strategically, and more lethally when deploying to hostile ZONES.

  

REMARK: Find your focus, but understand, each attribute improves your ability to survive against shifting odds and variable threats. Play with the stats. Craft YOUR perfect SHELL.
