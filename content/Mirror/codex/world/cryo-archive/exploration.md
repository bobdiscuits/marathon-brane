RUNNERS' GUIDE                                      v.1.001.03.tciv 

CRYO ARCHIVE REGION: CONTROL 

\=================================================================== 

LOCATION: UESC MARATHON 

ROLE: Central cryo operations hub. 

OVERVIEW: CONTROL sits at the heart of the CRYO ARCHIVE. All REGIONS within the ZONE connect here. CONTROL houses massive cryo tanks that provided each REGION with a regulated supply of cryofluids to ensure all colonist sleep units and their scheduled sleep cycles were maintained with strict adherence to UESC cryo survival guidelines. 

CONTROL connects PRESERVATION to INDEX and BIOSTOCK to CARGO, supporting the safe and secure transition from cryostasis to long-term storage. CONTROL also connects INDEX and CARGO to REVIVAL and STEERAGE for the seamless passage of sleeping colonists to their wake-cycling support experiences.
