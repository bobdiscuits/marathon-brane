DIRE MARSH \[NIGHT\] REGION: ALGAE PONDS

LOCATION: South Central Marsh

  

INSIGHT: ALGAE PONDS' central location makes it a dangerous REGION to explore. Beyond the general threat of UESC Security patrols and the rising concern over nocturnal phenomena across "NIGHT MARSH," any centralized REGION comes with heightened risk of rival RUNNER encounters as crews scavenge their way across a ZONE.

  

OVERVIEW: As a dedicated facility for active experimentation in curating sustainable food stuffs to help fuel colony longevity, ALGAE PONDS was a core element of DIRE MARSH's ag-hub responsibilities. What remains unclear, is the extent to which its placement between the ANOMALY and the QUARANTINE site affected its crops and when/if any tainted product was shipped beyond the ZONE.

  

NOTICE: A UESC NETWORK TOWER is located at the northern edge of ALGAE PONDS.

  

REMARK: High ground has many advantages. In the dark of night, an elevated POV allows a RUNNER to scout for distant illumination and adjust their intentions accordingly.

\===================================================================

  

LOCATION: East Marsh

  

INSIGHT: Newly converted base of operations for UESC Security Forces ANOMALY research.

NOTICE: A UESC NETWORK TOWER sits just south of COMPLEX's observation deck.

The mysteries of TAU CETI IV deepen by the run. And that's before we consider the truly alien possibilities we can no longer ignore.

DIRE MARSH \[NIGHT\] REGION: AI UPLINK

\===================================================================

  

LOCATION: Southeast Marsh

  

INSIGHT: AI UPLINK's main structure offers RUNNERS both an elevated view of the UESC's field research into the ANOMALY and access to the sub-level maintenance and secured support server facility. From scouting the dark expanse of the ZONE from the upper platform or rooftop to using clandestine pathways to traverse unseen, AI UPLINK offers unique value for scavenging and combat.

  

OVERVIEW: AI UPLINK remains dormant, and it appears the UESC's presence here is tied to their focused desire to keep the REGION offline. Signs of corruption and potential rampancy in the REGION's colony-era AI dictate great care is needed in monitoring the site. It is currently unclear if colony intelligence security protocols left the REGION barren or if any of the UPLINK intelligence operations are retained within the locked systems.

  

NOTICE: A UESC NETWORK TOWER has been erected near AI UPLINK's main operational structure.

  

REMARK: Corrupt systems rarely sleep peacefully.
