# Captain Orion

> UESC Captain, commanding officer of the **1st Tau Ceti Investigatory Unit** — a MIDA PYROCLAST target with a documented history of involvement in Martian and Lunar security operations, public advocacy for coercive interrogation tactics, and suspected ties to multiple atrocities and the deployment of an undisclosed military AI.

## What the source establishes — canon

**Callsign and position.** Orion is referred to by callsign only in the MIDA target file. His official position is listed as **Captain, 1st Tau Ceti Investigatory Unit**.[^1] The UESC memorandum Project:Goliath establishes the existence of an investigatory mission to Tau Ceti dispatched from Sol to re-establish contact with New Cascadia and investigate the disruption of communications,[^3] providing the institutional context for this unit, though Orion is not named directly in that document.

**MIDA classification.** Orion is designated a **PYROCLAST target** in MIDA's classified target file, shared by MIDA agent `_gantry` with Runners.[^1] The file is framed as intelligence for Runners operating against UESC interests: *"know thine enemy, runner."*[^1]

**Background as recorded in the MIDA file.** The MIDA personnel file lists the following history for Orion:[^1]

- Clashed with **APHELION Group** on Mars and Luna.
- Worked in high-security detention units on Luna, described in the file as *"notorious for cruelties like 'corrective isolation' and 'punitive force-feeding'."*
- Publicly advocates for the use of *"bio-augment manipulation"* as a *"persuasion tactic"* — glossed in the file as *"(read: legalized torture)."*
- *"Held the company line"* at the **Vallis Bohr Revolt**.
- *"Wiped up the blood"* at the **Jezero Crater Massacre**.
- Suspected ties to the **Children of Mars erasure conspiracy**.
- Suspected of deploying an **unregulated and publicly undisclosed military AI, Cerberus**, described as *"capable of mass violence."*[^1]

**Vulnerabilities as recorded in the MIDA file.** The same file notes two entries of insubordination during Orion's early UESC career, described as *"purged from the record when leadership swapped over after Jezero."* It also notes a *"personal relationship to MIDA agent '\_philomena', estranged."*[^1]

**Orion's own voice — message to Runners.** A recorded message attributed to Orion [ID: ORION] addresses Runners directly.[^2] In it, he defends UESC authority as a necessary cost of order for *"billions of civs living across Earth, Luna, and Mars,"* characterizes Runners as *"criminals"* who *"rigged the system"* and can *"die a hundred times and keep coming back"* while *"real people"* cannot, accuses MIDA of caring about *"power"* rather than Martian welfare, and invokes MIDA's historical death squads during their brief coup as evidence that the organization has not changed: *"People are the same."*[^2]

**Command of the Tau Ceti IV reclamation operation.** A UESC internal status report, authored and signed "REPORTER: ORION," establishes Orion as the officer reporting on-the-ground findings from the New Cascadia recovery effort.[^4] The report records: no colonist survivors found, with recovered human remains not matching population records even accounting for decay; colony AI nonfunctional, dormant, or inaccessible outside automatic subroutines, with colony records showing "severe malfunction across the entire suite"; salvageable lab equipment, vehicles, and agricultural infrastructure identified despite damage and decay; the UESC Marathon remaining in orbit with investigations stalled by repeated incidents (cross-referenced in the report as "Report: Casualty Roster," "Incident Report: Code Name Visitor," and "Incident Report: Code Name Tantalus Event"); corporate salvage-rights interference being actively blocked; and Runner "unauthorized salvage operations" described as "a significant impediment to on-the-ground operations" that deterrence tactics have failed to stop.[^4] Orion's appended note requests HQ backup to "run interference with corporate leadership" and confirms his unit is "enforcing New Cascadia as a restricted location" under full confidentiality.[^4]

**Authorizing analysis of Anomaly-contaminated materials over subordinate objection.** A UESC drone shipment manifest for cargo collected from "Incident 253" (cross-referenced as "Incident Report: Contagious Mimesis") — material judged severely contaminated after exposure to the Anomaly — records an internal dispute between two officials (`023_SYAR` and `015_ITRE`) over whether to proceed with immediate analysis or default to containment.[^5] `023_SYAR` reports that "per Orion, all analysis is paused" pending containment and eventual incineration, and separately questions whether "Orion back this" when ordered by HQ to begin analysis immediately, noting Orion's "initial instruction was risk mitigation." `015_ITRE` replies "Confirmed by Orion" — UESC personnel with hazardous materials training are to begin assessment in orbital lab units immediately. `023_SYAR`'s further objection ("this isn't a reliable indicator of stability... a high risk of another destructive incident") is overruled: "Denied. No further risk assessment is required or requested."[^5] The exchange shows Orion's stated risk-mitigation instruction being superseded — by HQ order, relayed and confirmed under his name — without his own direct voice appearing in the exchange.

**Public relations sessions with Officer Keleti.** A sequence of internal UESC recordings captures Orion drafting sanitized public messaging with First Officer Keleti [ID: TKEL], described by Orion as "my right hand in the field."[^6] Orion rejects Keleti's honest framing ("more questions than answers") as sounding "weak" and pushes for "something positive," prompting Keleti to reword findings as "costly but successful" recovery efforts.[^6] Orion attributes the messaging assignment to Admiral Sulera, who "believes you put a 'nicer face' on the operation. At least for PR."[^6] In a second session, Keleti pushes back sharply against the spin exercise, proposing unfiltered disclosures instead: *"the alien music box that fried Rossi. And you, Cap[tain]"*; *"the Cerberus hallucinations"*; *"the constant shell reprints"*; and that *"Runners are getting chewed up every time they set foot on the Marathon. Almost as bad as we are."*[^7] Orion overrules the impulse — *"The job is doing whatever it takes for the mission to succeed... now, the job is to tell a story that sounds good enough to keep our people supplied. I don't like it either. But we chose this."*[^7] — and the two settle on a sanitized line about the Marathon "remain[ing] in orbit" while "our people have been working... tirelessly... to secure it."[^7] A finished "Public Status Report" recording shows Orion delivering the polished result: describing the recovery as dealing with "ruins and remains much older than any typical investigation," citing "relentless criminal interference," and closing with the reassurance that "the UESC will bring order to Tau Ceti and preserve the legacy of New Cascadia."[^8]

**Being personally named as a Cerberus hallucination casualty.** Keleti's unfiltered list of things UESC is concealing from Sol names Orion directly alongside the "alien music box that fried Rossi": *"And you, Cap[tain]. What are the Cerberus hallucinations costing us? Or the constant shell reprints?"*[^7] This is the pack's only indication that Orion may be personally experiencing the Cerberus-linked hallucinations he is otherwise only associated with as an operator (see MIDA's "unregulated... military AI" allegation, above).[^1] The source does not clarify what "Cerberus hallucinations" are, whether they are shared by the wider team, or their relationship to Cerberus's described capacity for "mass violence."

## Source-silent / open questions

- Orion's full name is not given in any source. The pack contains only the callsign "Orion." Source-silent.
- The MIDA target file is an adversarial document compiled by MIDA operatives; its characterizations of Orion's history (Vallis Bohr, Jezero, APHELION, Cerberus) are stated as MIDA intelligence, not confirmed by neutral sources. The pack does not include corroborating UESC records for these specific events. Source-silent on independent confirmation.
- The identity of MIDA agent `_philomena` and the nature of her relationship to Orion are not elaborated. Source-silent.
- The AI referred to as **Cerberus** — described as *"unregulated and publicly undisclosed"* and *"capable of mass violence"* — does not appear elsewhere in the pack. Whether it was deployed at Tau Ceti, still operational, or connected to the Anomaly is source-silent.
- The **Children of Mars erasure conspiracy** is not described or explained further in any pack volume. Source-silent.
- The **APHELION Group** is not described beyond being the subject of a clash with Orion on Mars and Luna. Source-silent.
- Whether Orion arrived at Tau Ceti IV aboard the investigatory mission vessel, his current operational status in the present-day timeline, and his fate are source-silent.
- Orion's message to Runners is labeled [2] in the pack, implying at least one earlier message ([1]) that does not appear in the extracted Orion material; whether that message exists in the Mirror is not confirmed from this pack alone.
- Whether Orion is personally afflicted by the "Cerberus hallucinations" Keleti references, or is named only as the mission's responsible officer, is not resolved — the source raises this only in passing, in a list of things the UESC is concealing from Sol.[^7] Source-silent on symptoms, onset, or outcome.
- "Rossi," described as having been "fried" by "the alien music box," does not appear elsewhere in the extracted Orion material — identity and fate source-silent.[^7]
- The "Incident Report: Contagious Mimesis," "Incident Report: Code Name Visitor," "Incident Report: Code Name Tantalus Event," and "Report: Casualty Roster" documents are cross-referenced by title in Orion's status report and the drone manifest thread but are not themselves present in the extracted Orion material. Their content is source-silent from this hub's citations.
- Admiral Sulera's exact authority over Orion (direct superior vs. political sponsor) is implied by the PR-messaging exchanges but not formally established.[^6][^7][^8]
