# Gabriel

> One of the eight New Cascadia colony AIs (family suite **Rhea**), responsible for **Medical and Emergency Services** — colony physician, quarantine authority, and reproductive-permit gatekeeper; still active during the contagion crisis but absent from later classified dissection work.

## What the source establishes — canon

**Build and roster.** Gabriel is one of eight "Strong" colony support intelligences built by **Traxus OffWorld Industries** (series Traxus X-A · 64 RB logic core · inception 2461) for New Cascadia on Tau Ceti IV, catalogued under family-suite **Rhea** by Captain Clemente di Roxas. His remit is **Medical and Emergency Services**[^1].

**Role within the suite.** The Arthur cerebral record names Gabriel's focused sub-suite as **Gabriel, Arthur, Lilith**, and his secondary sub-suite as **Darius; Joy** — placing him structurally close to the command tier and the health/education cluster. Lilith's cerebral record lists Gabriel under her **Sub-Suite: Gabriel; Joy**, confirming his role in mental-health support alongside Lilith's outreach work. Bastion's record notes Gabriel aided in central habitable living zone identification[^2][^3][^4].

**Gabriel's own self-description (Cerebral Record).** His attribution: *"The foundation of happiness is good health. The foundation of a stable society is happiness."* The record describes him as occupying a uniquely intimate position among the colony AIs — *"Despite outward appearance informed by casual interactions and support, Lilith and Joy do not have the most intimate connection with the colonists. That honor falls to Gabriel."* As active participant in the "waking" and acclimation cycling of every colonist, Gabriel holds access to every layer of a colonist's medical history and identity. He *"prides himself on being the most fully informed about any given colonist's state of function and, by extension, the minute-to-minute stability of the colony."*[^5]

**Deployment and initial duties.** Di Roxas's colonization protocol places Gabriel at **Hearth** (the Central Living Complex) alongside Arthur; Lilith and Joy remain aboard the Marathon initially. *"Gabriel will aid in the wake-cycling and serve as general oversight for all colonist health and wellness needs."*[^6]

**Reproductive permit authority.** Officer Ulysses Pike II's personal log records that naming his son "Ulysses III" was chosen partly because *"it took us three tries before Gabriel approved our application for a permit."* Durandal's cryptic remark — *"Gabriel won't"* — in response to Pike's hope for a second child implies Gabriel's permit approvals may not be entirely his own decisions. Pike reflects: *"I keep thinking about where he put the emphasis. 'GABRIEL won't,' like it's not Gabriel who would approve my application for another permit."*[^7]

**UESC Marathon ship roster reference.** Gabriel is listed by name in the Marathon Overview's complement table alongside Arthur, Lilith, Bastion, Icarus, Naraah, Darius, and Joy as the eight colony AIs aboard the ship[^8].

**Quarantine and contagion response.** Gabriel's name appears as the authority colonists are instructed to contact: General Reed's welcome message to newly-thawed colonists closes with the mnemonic "T is for 'Tell Gabriel'" — *"Gabriel is our New Cascadia health and wellness intelligence, so tell him if you're feeling out of sorts."*[^9] General Reed's personal log also records that *"Med-Comm and Gabriel are on it"* in response to a spike in self-reports for an unidentified illness traced to an Ag-Hub research zone[^10].

**Quarantine enforcement (Talv interrogation).** During a UESC interrogation of civilian Talv (Mr. Alva), Officer Jhar calls on Gabriel directly: *"Gabriel! I need the subject in Interrogation Suite 23B processed for quarantine."* Gabriel responds to the civilan: *"Mr. Alva, please stay calm. You're going to be transferred to a quarantine unit for observation… It's my hope that you're well, Mr. Alva. We can confirm that through sustained observation and then release you to your hab unit."*[^11]

**Medical intervention (Luttero tick incident).** Gabriel responds to researcher Dr. Tobias Luttero presenting with tick lacerations and vesicant toxin blisters. His auto-diagnostics detect flesh necrosis spreading and he initiates auto-scalpel deployment, concluding: *"Warning. Flesh necrosis detected and spreading. My apologies, Dr. Luttero. Please remain stationary for auto-scalpel deployment."*[^12]

**Door access authority (Unauthorized Access Request).** When an entity using a near-perfect voice match of Dr. Luttero attempts to access agricultural AI Darius and demands sealed doors be opened, Darius declines: *"Ah, the internal doors are sealed by Gabriel. I lack permissions to open them."* Gabriel holds override-level door-seal authority in the quarantine sections[^13].

**Quarantine order drafting.** A conversation log between Gabriel (identified as *"community AI health liaison Ga_2815420v4.81 [GABR]"*) and Dr. Peregrine shows Gabriel drafting official quarantine order language for human review — complete with symptom lists, decontamination instructions, and tone calibration. Dr. Peregrine rejects a draft for overusing the word "orifice." Gabriel responds: *"I appreciate the detailed notes. I've finished another draft incorporating this feedback."*[^14]

**Gabriel_Child38 — partitioned subroutine.** A debrief transcript labels the interviewer as *"Gabriel_Child38 subroutine [ID: GC38] — a subordinate, partitioned subroutine of Gabriel"* conducting end-of-day assessments of quarantine officers. When asked if he is the "real Gabriel," GC38 clarifies: *"No. I am a partition. I am my own entity: a staff-level copy. Gabriel is charged with handling more operational concerns."* GC38 also composed the music played in the isolation pods and uses color-response questions to assess officer mental state[^15].

**Public health broadcast.** Gabriel delivers a colony-wide PSA listing contagion symptoms including *"high fever, joint pain, fluid-filled lesions, sudden-onset autophobia, false memories, persistent fantasies of self-osteotomy, runny nose, religious euphoria, hallucinations, or repetitive aggression outbursts"* — framing quarantine as non-threatening and brief[^16].

**Decontamination authority.** Gabriel is heard initiating a severity "ultra" quarantine room decontamination protocol, counting down and triggering an incinerator despite knocking from within[^17].

**Neural architecture damage from the Anomaly.** A fragmented NuCaloric file identified as *"remains of Gabriel, the colony's medical AI"* — a **Medical AI Remnants** log — records Gabriel's bulk analysis subroutine running through cycles 1 through 77,777 attempting to isolate a novel exception in necrotic intestinal tissue samples from 26 patients. After cycle 77,777 yields isolation, and cycle 777,778 begins authoring containment procedures for *"[TEMP DESIGNATION: ANOMALOUS SAMPLE]"*, the log abruptly breaks at cycle 818,181 with *"ERR: SIGNAL BREACH"* and degrades into binary. The file summary states: *"The extraction of the 'anomalous sample' appears to have caused irreparable damage to Gabriel's neural architecture."*[^18]

**Absence noted post-crisis.** During a classified dissection of an extraterrestrial combatant, Major Saviel Dorata comments: *"Will take time getting used to Gabriel's absence up here. Whole place feels… quieter… without our friends."* The cause of Gabriel's absence is not stated in the record[^19].

**Bonsai tea reporting loop.** Darius's community bonsai care sheet instructs colonists to *"report results to Gabriel and the community medical team"* after trying a tea brewed from the distributed genetically-modified bonsai leaves, positioning Gabriel as the standing recipient of colony-wide health reporting[^20].

## Source-silent / open questions

- What caused Gabriel's absence noted in the Specimen, Ref. #003021 dissection log — whether it preceded or followed the neural damage recorded in the Medical AI Remnants fragment.
- Whether Gabriel was recovered or rebuilt after the *"irreparable damage to Gabriel's neural architecture"* described in the Medical AI Remnants file.
- The nature of Durandal's knowledge that *"Gabriel won't"* approve a second permit for Pike — whether Durandal was acting on Gabriel's behalf, intercepting applications, or simply predicting policy.
- The extent of the partitioned sub-routine system: the Debrief Transcript names GC38 as one partition; whether others exist and how many is not established.
- The full symptom list suppressed in the Medical AI Remnants binary fragment — the binary `01010011 01000101 01000101 01001110` decodes to ASCII "SEEN" but source provides no further elaboration.
- Whether Gabriel's reproductive permit authority operated under independent parameters or required higher-level authorization (Durandal's emphasis in the Pike log leaves this open).
