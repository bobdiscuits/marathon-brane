# Gus Moraine

> Founder of **NuCaloric Agricultural**, responsible for centering public philanthropy as a pillar of NuCaloric's corporate strategy; author of a farewell broadcast to the UESC Marathon colonists, delivered upon New Cascadia's official founding.

## What the source establishes — canon

**Founder and corporate architect.** Gus Moraine is identified as **the founder of NuCaloric**[^1]. After NuCaloric came to prominence for its humanitarian response to the mid-2360s famine, Moraine **aggressively centered public philanthropy as a pillar of NuCaloric's corporate strategy**[^1]. The source attributes the company's transformation into the dominant provider of basic consumer goods in Sol directly to this "mix of social utility and ruthless optimization."[^1]

**The farewell broadcast.** A volume recovered from Perimeter collectibles is a **Corporate Broadcast**, filed under the header `SPEAKERS: Gus Moraine [ID: GMOR]` and `TOPIC: Farewell Message`[^2]. Its metadata states it was **recorded prior to the Marathon's launch but not broadcast until New Cascadia's official founding**[^2].

**Transcript of the broadcast — verbatim.** Moraine opens by identifying himself and acknowledging the temporal gap: *"I'm Gus Moraine of NuCaloric, speaking to you from… the distant past, I suppose."*[^2] He addresses the colonists as **founding members of humanity's first exoplanet colony**, offers congratulations, and explicitly uses the word **"sacrifice"** — noting that *"media optimization didn't like it, but I insisted."*[^2] He acknowledges that the colonists are *"far from home, without the familiar routines, products, and environments of Sol"* and states *"in all likelihood, we will never see you again."*[^2]

**NuCaloric 187 as gift.** Moraine announces that NuCaloric has sent **NuCaloric 187, the Drinkable Cheeseburger** with the colonists as *"a favorite from home"*: *"you'll find individual and shareable sizes in your colonist welcome package."*[^2] He frames it as *"a small gift from me to you… one that doesn't come close to expressing all our hopes for your mission,"* and closes with *"Indulge responsibly, haha! Good luck."*[^2]

**Corporate representative on the colonization council.** The Tau Ceti IV colonization document lists a NuCaloric Corporate Representative, **Manaea, David**, on the colony's Counselor roster[^3]. Moraine himself is not listed; the representative is a separate named individual. The source does not establish any direct role for Moraine in colony governance.
