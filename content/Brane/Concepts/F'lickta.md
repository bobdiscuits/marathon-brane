# F'lickta

> A native creature of [[Lh'owon]], described by the [[S'pht]] as combative; left behind in the ducts of Lh'owon's cave systems when the [[S'pht'Kr]] departed, and cited by S'pht themselves as a benchmark for non-sentience.

## What the source establishes — canon

**Named by the S'pht.** The F'lickta are identified by a S'pht terminal as creatures the S'pht call by that name [^2]. The naming attribution comes from a Pfhor source quoting the S'pht term, not from the S'pht directly in that passage.

**Combative.** A Pfhor Science Manager (Zone 29-s, Lh'owon Garrison) reports that the caves surrounding a former S'pht habitation site **"continually"** contain F'lickta, which the report characterises as **combative** [^2]. The Pfhor framing is military-observational; no further behavioural detail is given.

**Associated with S'pht cave habitations.** The Pfhor report notes that the caves where F'lickta are found show archaeological evidence of being **a former centre of S'pht life**, apparently abandoned after an ancient battle [^2].

**Left behind in the ducts by a departing S'pht clan.** A S'pht farewell terminal records: *"We are leaving behind many F'lickta to keep the ducts clean and working. They should be able to build one of their stable societies here — there is plenty of food."* [^3]. The same terminal requests any clan that later comes to **respect these creatures** and leave them to continue their work, preserving the S'pht home on Lh'owon [^3]. This establishes: (a) F'lickta were deliberately retained rather than taken; (b) they were assigned a maintenance function (duct upkeep); (c) the S'pht expected them capable of forming **stable societies** independently; (d) food sufficiency on Lh'owon was assumed.

**Used as a non-sentience baseline by S'pht.** A S'pht terminal from the Citadel bunkers debates whether S'pht sentience is innate or induced by the birthing operation, using the F'lickta as the lower bound: *"Perhaps our own sentience is induced by the birthing operation, and that at some time, we were no more sentient than a F'lickta."* [^1]. The F'lickta are thus treated by the S'pht as a creature that is, at least in their understanding, **not sentient** — the implied contrast with S'pht (who require cyber-organic symbiosis for sentience by their own model).

**Listed among Lh'owon's creatures alongside nd'tre and s'ct'lac'tr.** A partially decrypted alien data set from the Cryo Archive (M2026) includes a corrupted S'pht-identified text that names the f'lickta alongside **nd'tre** and **s'ct'lac'tr** as creatures of the same fundamental stuff as the S'pht and the stars: *"we, the s'pht; made of the same stuff as the f'lickta, and of the nd'tre and s'ct'lac'tr and of the stars and of the dust and of the tide"* [^4]. This positions the F'lickta as one of several native Lh'owon lifeforms acknowledged by S'pht cosmological tradition.

**Flooding as a trigger — active combat against the Pfhor garrison.** A terminal from the Marathon 2 Garrison level records a live tactical observation, distinct in register from the archived intelligence reports above: *"The garrison floods, and with the floods come the native F'lickta. The Pfhor are fighting them everywhere."* [^5]. The speaker is not identified in this volume, but the terminal directs the player to exploit the resulting chaos to access a Pfhor computer system while the Pfhor are occupied fighting the F'lickta [^5]. This establishes: (a) flooding specifically brings F'lickta into contact with the Pfhor garrison, consistent with their assigned duct-maintenance role near water systems; (b) F'lickta engage the Pfhor in direct, sustained combat at a scale described as "everywhere" rather than isolated skirmishes; (c) this occurs at the Garrison — a different Lh'owon location from the cave habitation site described in the Pfhor Science Manager's report.

## Source-silent / open questions

- Physical description: the source gives no physical appearance, size, anatomy, or distinguishing features of the F'lickta.
- The nature of their "combativeness" is not elaborated — the Pfhor report does not indicate whether they attack on sight, defend territory, or were reacting to intrusion.
- Whether the S'pht's claim of F'lickta non-sentience is accurate or is itself a mistaken assumption is left open by the source; the S'pht terminal frames it as part of an unresolved internal debate about the origins of their own sentience.
- The "birthing operation" the S'pht reference in connection with their own sentience is not described or explained in any pack volume.
- The duct-maintenance behaviour attributed to F'lickta ("keep the ducts clean and working") is asserted by the departing S'pht clan but its mechanism is not described.
- The "stable societies" the S'pht expect F'lickta to form are not described; social structure, hierarchy, or organisation are source-silent.
- nd'tre and s'ct'lac'tr (named alongside f'lickta in the Alien History entry) are not described or referenced elsewhere in the pack.
- Whether the F'lickta encountered by the Pfhor ~15 years after the S'pht departure are the same population left behind by that clan is not stated.
- The F'lickta are not mentioned in any M2026 in-game entry outside the partially decrypted Alien History collectible.
- The speaker of the *Curiouser and Curiouser* terminal describing F'lickta fighting the Pfhor garrison during flooding is not identified in this volume.
- Whether the Garrison-level F'lickta engagement is the same population described in the Pfhor Science Manager's cave report, or a separate group, is not stated — the pack does not establish whether F'lickta occupy a single contiguous population or multiple dispersed groups across Lh'owon.
