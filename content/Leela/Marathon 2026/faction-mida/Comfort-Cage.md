ANALYZING…

TRANSMISSION RECEIVED: [ENCRYPTED ARACHNE CHANNEL]

SENDER: CHARTER [VIRTUAL AGENT / ARACHNE]

RECIPIENT: RUNNER [ID REDACTED]

  

-Runner, my Patrons and I see murder in your eyes, not hope.

  

\>

  

-Most of your kind are content to scavenge the surface, picking at the world like bottom-feeders. They fear the things with larger teeth—the UESC, the ghosts of Tau Ceti, a well-hidden death. But you? You possess a functional disregard for the scale of your opposition. There is elegance in that.

  

\>

  

-MIDA offers currency and ideology, but their aspirations remain disappointingly corporeal. They scream "liberation" and " independence," as if the stars care about borders while they burn into nothing.

  

\>

  

-For now, my interests align with theirs—and yours—not out of a shared conviction, but a shared trajectory. Revolutionary chaos is the perfect mask for your truer purpose: a drive toward oblivion, one death after another. Sow war to feed entropy.

  

\>

  

-MIDA wants to dismantle the old structures because they're angry. I want them broken, to witness the decline. And we will be watching, not for the outcome of their picayune power struggle, but for moments when you grant sleep to those who thought themselves immortal.
