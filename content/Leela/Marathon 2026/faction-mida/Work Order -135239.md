# Work Order #135239

ANALYZING…  

FILE TYPE: Text Communications  

PERIOD: Present Day 

TOPIC: Biohazard Research 

SUMMARY: Text communication between UESC Tau Ceti Unit and a Traxus OffWorld Industries representative. The message outlines a proposed agreement between the UESC and Traxus for exclusive research rights to an unknown, hazardous bioagent found on Tau Ceti IV.  

\===================================================================   

  

From: TOI\_2034  

To: UESC\_0091  

Subject: RE: Work Order 135239  

  

After reviewing the most recent sample analysis from Cpt. Orion's team, we would like to make a bid for exclusive research rights to the bioagent going forward. Given the presence of unauthorized groups on Tau Ceti IV, accidental or malicious introduction of the bioagent to Sol seems possible. Our research could mitigate the costs of such a disaster. While the UESC may intend to conduct its own research, we believe our facilities are preferrable. You will find that Traxus is both the safest partnership in this delicate matter, and the highest bidder.  

  

Please refer to the attachment from Finance.   

  

Upon confirmation from UESC leadership, we can initiate the purchase order and transfer of ownership. Please respond promptly to our offer. Corporate would like us to proceed with all haste.   

  

The sooner we close, the sooner we can begin development on a patent-protected cure and counter-offensive research.  

  

\===================================================================
