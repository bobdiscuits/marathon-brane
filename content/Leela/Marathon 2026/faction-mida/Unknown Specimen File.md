# Unknown Specimen File

ANALYZING…   

FILE TYPE: UESC Internal Report   

PERIOD: Present Day 

TOPIC: Hazardous Specimen 

SUMMARY: Classified report detailing the key attributes of a hazardous specimen unit discovered by the UESC in the ruins of the New Cascadian colony. Several key details have been redacted by the UESC.  

\===================================================================  

  

File: UNKNOWN SPECIMEN UNIT #325  

  

Description: A Class-L specimen containment unit, standard among colony supplies. Unit is damaged, deformed along its seal and with visible external wear. The container appears to be leaking, though there is no discernable puncture. The substance within is of an unknown composition. It is suspected to be [REDACTED] in origin, contained by UESC officials during the colony's operation after the [REDACTED] event. The unit occasionally emits sound.  

  

Additional Details: The substance inside the unit distorts the area around it visually. The substance fluctuates in size and appearance but follows no clear rules in its fluctuations. The auditory experience of the unit differs depending on the observer. It has been described variously as "like music," "like a wasp's nest," "like a familiar voice," and "like a migraine." One officer has been medically quarantined after contact and several others detained for psychological observation.  

  

Protocol: Use extreme caution. Unit must be contained if it can be approached safely.
