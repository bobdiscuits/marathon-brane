PERIMETER REGION: SOUTH RELAY

\=================================================================== 

  

LOCATION: Southwest Edge

  

ROLE: A secondary support RELAY built once it was realized that planetary readings collected by the DATA WALL were too dense for a single RELAY to filter. 

  

OVERVIEW: SOUTH RELAY is an incomplete twin. It appears to have been constructed more efficiently than NORTH RELAY, but was never finished. Slightly elevated levels of decay hint at the southern reach of PERIMETER either being less maintained in the colony's waning days, or at some as yet unregistered side effect of the readings they were attempting to capture here.
