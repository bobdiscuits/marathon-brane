OPPORTUNITY: NETWORK TOWERS

GOAL: Access, identify, loot

RISK: UESC Security Forces, Runner interference

REWARD: Supplies, weapons, gear

OVERVIEW: The UESC has established a series of NETWORK TOWERS across DIRE MARSH. Accessing and hacking the TOWERS can allow RUNNERS to pinpoint UESC stockpiles of goods gathered from across the ZONE, including useful items scavenged from RUNNERS—either left behind or lost in combat. 

  

     • LOCATE a TOWER

     • HACK the TOWER

     • IDENTIFY a cache 

     • GET to cache 

     • LOOT cache

     • SURVIVE

  

TOWER locations have been identified near the following REGIONS:

  

     • COMPLEX [south end]

     • AI UPLINK

     • BIO-RESEARCH [north end]

     • ALGAE PONDS [north end]

     • MAINTENANCE

     • WEST GATE

REMARK: Cache access requires UESC CERTS. Defeat UESC Security Forces to acquire CERTS.
