ROLE[S]: Field operations command unit; High value site defense; Target immobilization and elimination; Threat awareness and targeted engagement

OVERVIEW: The UESC's FROST WARDENS are elite Scout Commander combat units stationed as oversight defense for critical operation sites. Their enhanced perception matrix is augmented by an attached TrueSight Individual Mechanics Platform [IMP] that allows the FROST WARDEN to track and identify threats within its jurisdiction.

  

The FROST WARDEN's main deterrents are deployable FROST MINES that create a dangerous combat space for any trespassers. RUNNERS impacted by FROST detonations are slowed and vulnerable to additional attacks, giving the FROST WARDEN and its support units an advantage they rarely let slip.

  

OFFENSIVE / DEFENSIVE CAPABILITIES: 

     • Increased armor for improved durability 

     • Precision targeting

     • TrueSight threat perception IMP 

     • FROST MINE area control explosives

     • Heat burst personal proximity defense
