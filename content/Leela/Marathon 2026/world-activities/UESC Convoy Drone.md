# UESC Convoy Drone

rnnrqterm.cdx.access                        - -:- -.- -.- -:- -.- -

\=================================================================== 

RUNNERS' GUIDE                                      v.1.001.07.tciv

SCAVENGING: UESC CONVOY DRONE

\=================================================================== 

  

OPPORTUNITY: UESC CONVOY DRONE 

THREAT: UESC ASSAULT Troops, Rival RUNNERS 

RISK: Distraction can lead to unexpected trouble 

  

REWARD: High value loot 

  

OVERVIEW: The UESC CONVOY DRONE ferries high-value items scavenged from TAU CETI and RUNNERS. The DRONE itself has a defensive shielding that only opens on its underside at checkpoints on its journey across a ZONE. It is also guarded by ASSAULT Troops for added protection against RUNNER theft.  

  

Best option is to take out the guards before the DRONE reaches a checkpoint, then engage the DRONE itself.  

  

Successfully destroying the DRONE will drop its payload for any RUNNER to loot [so watch your back] 

     • OBSERVE CONVY DRONE 

     • TRACK it across the ZONE 

     • ELIMINATE guards 

     • ATTACK DRONE'S underside when vulnerable 

     • DESTROY DRONE 

     • LOOT payload 

  

REMARK: Also works as bait. Stalk and pounce, if you're feeling opportunistic.
