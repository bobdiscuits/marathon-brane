rnnrqterm.cdx.access                        - -:- -.- -.- -:- -.- -

\=================================================================== 

RUNNERS' GUIDE                                      v.1.001.07.tciv      

SCAVENGING: UESC TRANSPORT DRONE 

\=================================================================== 

  

OVERVIEW: UESC Transport Drones are small, aerial cargo transports utilized by UESC Security Forces on Tau Ceti IV to ferry salvage from the colony and defeated Runners to off-zone collection sites for transfer to UESC command vessels in orbit for assessment and cataloging. 

  

Transport Drones tend to carry a variety of items—resources, materials, weapons—and they are lightly shielded, so don't be afraid to shoot them out of the sky to check their loot if you feel safe making a little noise while on a run. 

  

REMARK: Free loot is free loot, unless another crew is in earshot of your pop shots. Careful out there, Runners. Greed is good, until it's not.
