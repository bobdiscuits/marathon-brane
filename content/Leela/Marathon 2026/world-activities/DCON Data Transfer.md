# DCON Data Transfer

rnnrqterm.cdx.access                        - -:- -.- -.- -:- -.- -

\=================================================================== 

RUNNERS' GUIDE                                      v.1.001.07.tciv 

SCAVENGING: DCON DATA TRANSFER DEVICE

\=================================================================== 

  

TOOL: Deconstructive Data Transfer [DCON] 

USE: Limited use, secure surface-to-orbit item transfer 

RISK: Momentary vulnerability while activating 

REWARD: Send requested item to interested FACTION without exfil 

  

OVERVIEW: Deconstructive [DCON] Data Transfer Devices are specialized teleportation devices that allow a user to send atomized data between two points, typically from a planetary surface to orbit. DCONs have intricate signal encryption, allowing them to maintain a connection between sender and receiver regardless of atmospheric or dedicated signal interference. RUNNERS utilize DCONs to skirt TAU CETI IV's unique energy readings and subvert UESC comms scramblers to deliver data of interest to FACTIONS willing to pay good credits for TCIV's secrets. 

  

     • ACQUIRE data of interest to a FACTION. 

     • LOCATE a DCON. 

     • INPUT data to initiate transfer. 

     • PROFIT! 

  

REMARK: Have your crew watch your back. Or, if solo running, grow eyes in the back of your head.
