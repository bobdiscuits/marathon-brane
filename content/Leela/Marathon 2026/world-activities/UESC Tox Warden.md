RUNNERS' GUIDE                                      v.1.001.05.tciv

THREATS: UESC TOX WARDEN 

\=================================================================== 

  

TYPE: TOX WARDEN [GRENADIER] 

AFFIL: Unified Earth Space Council [UESC]  

MODEL: Autonomous Robotic Soldier [ARS]  

CONFIG: Elite Chem-Warfare Combat Command Platform [EC3P]  

MANUF: UESC Combat Robotics Division, Mars  

SERIAL: EC3PXXXXXXXXXX  

  

\=================================================================== 

  

ROLE[S]: Field operations command unit; Chemical combat deterrent; High-lethality area defense   

  

OVERVIEW: TOX WARDENS are specialized UESC GRENADIER combat platforms designed to serve as an elite field command unit. The TOX WARDEN's arsenal is engineered to deliver toxic payloads to disperse or eliminate enemy threats. Their use of chemical warfare in defense of the UESC's fragile jurisdiction in the TAU CETI system is another example of the UESC's willingness to forego established Rules of Engagement.  

  

OFFENSIVE / DEFENSIVE CAPABILITIES: 

     • Increased armor for improved durability 

     • Hyper-vigilant aggression systems 

     • Enhanced M77 Assault Rifle 

     • Ranged grenade barrage w/ enhanced immobilizing munitions 

     • Specialized grenade arm 

            • Launches toxic chem grenades 

                   • TOX WARDEN's chem grenades release a hazardous

                     chemtrail along their trajectory and a toxic

                     explosion at the point of detonation

  

KNOWN DEPLOYMENT[S]:  

     • OUTPOST [DORMITORIES] 

  

REMARK: TOX WARDENS are built to withstand the toxic effects of their weaponry, but their support troops ARE NOT. Use this to your advantage, if you can survive a face-to-face run-in long enough to strategize.
