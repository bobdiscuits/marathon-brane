# UESC Quarantine Standards

ANALYZING…  

FILE TYPE: Encrypted UESC Alert 

PERIOD: Colony Era 

TOPIC: Quarantine Enforcement 

SUMMARY: Encrypted alert from General Reed to UESC quarantine enforcement, sent shortly before quarantine instructions for the general public. Annotation by unknown UESC soldier. 

\=================================================================== 

  

CONFIDENTIAL: QUARANTINE SELECTION AND ENFORCEMENT 

  

For colonists who are mildly ill with ONLY physical symptoms: 

  

- Isolation can be discontinued in two weeks if physical symptoms do not mutate 

- Full monitoring PPE should be worn at home and in public spaces for an additional two weeks 

- If physical symptoms mutate, the two-week isolation period must reset 

- If psychological disturbances are observed, proceed to the following set of instructions. 

  

For colonists who are moderately ill or display any psychological disturbances: 

  

- Isolation CANNOT BE DISCONTINUED without military sanction 

- Treat isolation ward as a CLASS B mutation site for security purposes, including the use of automated enforcement 

- Serial testing is required for ongoing contagion research 

  

For colonists who are severely ill with novel physical and psychological symptoms:  

  

- Isolation CANNOT BE DISCONTINUED under present circumstances 

  

- Restrict all communications except with authorized medical staff
