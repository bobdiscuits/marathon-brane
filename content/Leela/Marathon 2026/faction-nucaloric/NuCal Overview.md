FACTION: NUCALORIC AGRICULTURAL [NuCal]

ROLE: Philanthropically minded consumer megacorp

CLASSIFICATION: Megacorporation

AGENT: Gaius

  

OVERVIEW: NUCALORIC came to prominence for its humanitarian response to a devastating famine in the 2360s. The company's founder, Gus Moraine, aggressively centered public philanthropy as a pillar of NUCALORIC's corporate strategy. This mix of social utility and ruthless optimization transformed NUCALORIC into the dominant provider of basic consumer goods in Sol, diversifying beyond agriculture to everything from hab units to cosmetics.

  

NUCALORIC signed a contract with the UESC to supply civilian goods for the UESC MARATHON, making them a legal stakeholder in the colony venture. However, their attempts to enforce salvage rights on TAU CETI IV are presently limited in scope. NUCALORIC does not appear interested in sustained confrontation with the UESC or other corporate stakeholders, preferring to gather historical data and specific intel using hired RUNNER operatives.
