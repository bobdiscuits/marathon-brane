# Lab Report: Novel Contagion

ANALYZING…   

FILE TYPE: Status Report 

PERIOD: Colony Era 

SPEAKERS: Song, C. [ID: CSNG], Wells, M. [ID: MWEL] 

TOPIC: Contagion Research 

SUMMARY: A recording of Dr. Clarissa Song and her technical assistant, Molly Wells, discussing novel contagion research. 

\=================================================================== 

  

[CSNG]: This is… Dr. Clarissa Song, beginning status report. My eyelids feel like sandpaper. Limbs are heavy. Heart is heavy. 

  

[CSNG]: This morning, we split one set of rations between us. Algae tea. Paste, the cheese flavor… 

  

[MWEL]: I like the honey oat one. Tastes more like breakfast should, you know? 

  

[CSNG]: I… I have with me Molly Wells, my tech assistant. 

  

[MWEL]: Not to interrupt, but could I take a look at the newest contagion analysis? 

  

[CSNG]: Ah, sure, if you can make sense of it. 

  

[MWEL]: This rate of neomorphic mutation is… 

  

[CSNG]: Impossible? Yeah, that's what I thought. But every analysis gives the same result. 

  

[CSNG]: What's more, two centrifuges are offline after leaking superheated gas. There was nothing inside that could've caused such a strong exothermic reaction. Just the contagion samples. 

  

[MWEL]: Must be a maintenance oversight. I'll request a crew to— 

  

[CSNG]: Ah, beat you to it, kiddo. Already checked the logs; everything was perfectly normal before those samples went in. 

  

[MWEL]: So… what? Our tiny volume of samples spontaneously combusted? 

  

[CSNG]: They did today. A week ago, we had software errors every time we tried to run analysis. A week before that, samples congealing into non-Newtonian fluids. Every result is impossible and impossible to replicate. 

  

[MWEL]: Can't replicate anything, can't form a coherent theory—we're no closer than we were at the start! At this rate, it'll take a miracle. 

  

[CSNG]: Well, I'm short on those. I hope nobody believed the propaganda on our way here. Colonizing an exoplanet was never gonna be easy, but Tau Ceti… 

  

[CSNG]: Maybe it's more than we can handle. Maybe it doesn't want us here. 

  

[MWEL]: You sound like Darius. 

  

[CSNG]: Well, before the admins took him offline. 

  

[MWEL]: Let's go for a walk. 

  

[CSNG]: You think I'm going crazy. 

  

[MWEL]: I think you've been staring at that screen all night. Let's stretch those legs. I have coffee back in my room. 

  

[CSNG]: You're kidding. Who did you murder for coffee rations? 

  

[MWEL]: Nobody important. Come on. Maybe Tau Ceti doesn't want you here, but I do. 

  

[CSNG]: Molly… 

  

[MWEL]: What? Why are you looking at me like that? 

  

[CSNG]: Your nose. It's bleeding.
