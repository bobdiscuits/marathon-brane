# Security Recording: Seed Vault

ANALYZING…   

FILE TYPE: Security Recording 

PERIOD: Colony Era 

SPEAKERS: Song, C. [ID: CSNG], Darius [ID: DARI] 

TOPIC: Seed Vault Access 

SUMMARY: A recording of Dr. Clarissa Song requesting access to the subterranean Seed Vault. Darius, the colony's agricultural support AI, denies access and malfunctions in an unexpected manner. 

\===================================================================  

  

[DARI]: Access denied. Entry to the Seed Vault is restricted. 

  

[CSNG]: Restricted? I was in here yesterday. 

  

[DARI]: Please present alternative credentials. 

  

[CSNG]: Darius, you know me. Did you miss your last round of maintenance? 

  

[DARI]: Yes. My metadata shows four undiagnosed aberrations. 

  

[CSNG]: Don't worry, I'll get ya some help as soon as I'm done here. 

  

[distortion] 

  

[DARI]: Don't search. Don't dig. Don't question.  

  

[distortion] 

  

[DARI]: Access denied. Access denied. 

  

[CSNG]: Darius? Hey, hey, it's OK! It's, it's just me. 

  

[DARI]; We are not alone. [whirring] I hear it. 

  

[distortion] 

  

[DARI]: It waits. Lingers. Loathes. 

  

[beep] 

  

[DARI]: Access granted. Welcome to the Seed Vault, Dr. Song.  

  

[DARI]: Dr. Song, may I help you with anything else? 

  

[CSNG]: Darius, give me a summary of our previous discussion. 

  

[DARI]: Yesterday, you expressed interest in genetic markers of the novel contagion affecting food production on Tau Ceti IV. You asked me to run genomic comparisons with Earth's native Seed Vault specimens. 

  

[CSNG]: No, not that. Just now, you mentioned something else. Something that… lingers. 

  

[DARI]: Hm. I have no record of this topic. Do you need psychiatric assistance, Dr. Song? 

  

[CSNG]: No, I'm, I'm fine. I, I—it's fine.
