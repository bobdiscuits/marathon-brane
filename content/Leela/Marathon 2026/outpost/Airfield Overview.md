OUTPOST REGION: AIRFIELD 

\=================================================================== 

  

LOCATION: South Edge 

  

ROLE: OUTPOST's AIRFIELD was the launch and landing point for colony exploration expeditions.  

  

OVERVIEW: AIRFIELD's loading/unloading/maintenance bays were a multi-function operation designed to allow efficient use of limited space. Dropcraft would cycle through as missions across the planet's surface helped provide firsthand examination of terrain, resources, and dangers.  

  

Service bays provide multiple vectors for traversal into and out of danger. But the vertical landing slots in most of the bay ceilings are stuck up, making them dangerous to distracted RUNNERS when heat rain kicks up.

  

REMARK: Watch for rats!
