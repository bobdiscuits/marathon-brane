OUTPOST REGION: PROCESSING 

\=================================================================== 

  

LOCATION: West Edge 

  

ROLE: Planetary research sample processing. 

  

OVERVIEW: OUTPOST's PROCESSING facilities oversaw the intake, assessment, and distribution of samples collected by the expeditions deployed by OUTPOST command directives. Every drone, dropcraft, rover, and research vehicle cycled through PROCESSING upon returning from field service.  

  

This REGION's low ground provides a striking view of the vast dryland flats that stretch into the distance. But the looming PINWHEEL and open expanses between structures makes navigating PROCESSING a dangerous proposition—gunfire and or literal rainfire will punish many RUNNERS caught off their guard.
