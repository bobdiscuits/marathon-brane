FACTION: SEKIGUCHI GENETICS [SekGen]

ROLE: Advanced bioengineering, research, and development

CLASSIFICATION: Megacorporation

AGENT: Nona

  

OVERVIEW: SEKIGUCHI GENETICS is a megacorporation dedicated to the imagination and creation of the future. As part of the larger Sekiguchi Group, SEKGEN is focused on cloneware, neural syncing, biological modifications like vision augmentation and epidermal reinforcement, and iteration on biocybernetic SHELLS.

  

SEKGEN has a practical monopoly on the SHELL market, from neural synchronization to the SHELLS themselves and the process for quickly manufacturing replacements. However, as SHELL technology spreads in usage, SekGen is facing new threats to its dominance. To combat the ever-rising tide of competition, SEKGEN is offering any RUNNER willing to risk the journey to TAU CETI opportunities to profit in exchange for their discoveries and any data they can collect from SHELL activity on a hostile, alien world.
