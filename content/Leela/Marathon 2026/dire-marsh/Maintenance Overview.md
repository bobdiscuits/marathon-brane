DIRE MARSH REGION: MAINTENANCE

LOCATION: Southwest Marsh 

  

ROLE: MAINTENANCE hub for the ZONE's fleet of field-operations vehicles—harvesters, threshers, drones—that work the fields stretching beyond the ZONE's borders.  

  

OVERVIEW: The main hangar structure houses service bays for the automated heavy field machinery that worked the fields. The exterior wings are charging and refill stations.
