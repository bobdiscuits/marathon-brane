RUNNERS' GUIDE                                      v.1.001.03.tciv     

DIRE MARSH REGION: ALGAE PONDS 

\=================================================================== 

  

LOCATION: South Central Marsh 

  

ROLE: Controlled algae and protein research facility focused on Tau Ceti-native specimens, edibility and sustainability. 

  

OVERVIEW: ALGAE PONDS's goal was the discovery or creation of a food source from native sources to supplement and eventually replace Sol-based reserves. Shallow grow ponds, research labs and dedicated storage/control units make up the majority of the facilities.  

  

The REGION's central location and raised platform provide a strategic vantage for RUNNERS either on the hunt for rival targets or simply looking to scout nearby REGIONS for threats to avoid.
