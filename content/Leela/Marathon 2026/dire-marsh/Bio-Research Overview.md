# Bio-Research Overview

rnnrqterm.cdx.access                        - -:- -.- -.- -:- -.- -

\=================================================================== 

RUNNERS' GUIDE                                      v.1.001.03.tciv     

DIRE MARSH REGION: BIO-RESEARCH 

\=================================================================== 

  

LOCATION: Northeast Marsh 

  

ROLE: Research and development dedicated to the study of Tau Ceti IV's native plant life and its potential for pharmaceutical use / breakthroughs. 

  

OVERVIEW: It's still too early in the rediscovery of NEW CASCADIA to grasp the full intentions or ramifications of the work done at DIRE MARSH's BIO-RESEARCH labs. Incubation tubs, biocycling outstations, hazmat requirements and mandatory delousing protocols all speak to the advanced but delicate nature of the work done here.  

  

The scientists who ran these facilities put theoretical medical research into practice. Even surface-level consideration of the active study of alien flora's potential for pharma-applications in human subjects raises a combination of awe and concern. In a colony of only 30,000+ people, each individual is important to the daily function and long-term survival of the endeavor. Begging the questions, where and how were the new medicines proposed here tested safely, and how were colonists chosen to participate?
