RUNNERS' GUIDE                                      v.1.001.03.tciv     

DIRE MARSH REGION: QUARANTINE 

\=================================================================== 

  

LOCATION: West Marsh 

  

ROLE: Contagion consequence mitigation complete with isolation pods and diagnostic labs.  

  

OVERVIEW: The fact a full-scale QUARANTINE containment tent with rows of isolation booths was erected in immediate proximity to one of the colony's dedicated food science research and grow ZONES makes it hard not to speculate about causes and effects and the eventual end fate of everyone connected with this ZONE. Theories and rumors won't provide answers, and the UESC's efforts to defend this REGION and ZONE only heighten the connection between the mysteries of whatever contagion they feared enough to trigger emergency containment protocols. 

  

Deployment of a temp QUARANTINE site alone suggests a dire need to address the contagion. The state of the facility—beyond abandonment—says whatever bad days happened here were a threat the colony took deadly serious. 

  

REMARK: The state of QUARANTINE raises more questions than its presence answers. And the history of high-attach viral spreads across Sol [the Red Sands Outbreak; the Neo-Tranquility Derma-Meltation; the Syrrl-93] paint a grim picture if a plague-level sickness hit the colony.
