RUNNERS' GUIDE                                      v.1.001.07.tciv

SCAVENGING: ALIEN ALLOY 

\=================================================================== 

  

OVERVIEW: A "living metal" alloy apparently unique to Tau Ceti IV. Shows metallic and biologic markers, allowing it to grow, heal, and adapt under certain conditions.

  

USE[S]: Research, construction, weaponry

  

REMARK: Alloy samples are safe to transport for short periods, but prolonged storage is not recommended due to its capacity for growth. Consumption of surroundings has been observed, as has unmotivated manipulation of environment that mimics sentient play behavior.
