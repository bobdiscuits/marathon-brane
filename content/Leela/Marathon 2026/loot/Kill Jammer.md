RUNNERS' GUIDE                                      v.1.001.02.tciv

RUNNER TACTICS: KILL JAMMER

\=================================================================== 

  

SIGNAL FRY: Information is a weapon like any other. In the right hands, it is as lethal as any firearm. Removing it from an encounter, even temporarily, can shift an advantage. That is the true value of a SIGNAL JAMMER—it changes perception in your favor. When active, your enemies have limited access to the full facts of the fight as you avoid ping indicators meant to mark your location, or simply confuse their optics for the instant you need to gain the upper hand. The scrambled read of incoming data shifts their awareness in ways that confuses reality and puts them on their heels. Few things are as deadly.  

  

For seasoned RUNNERS, a SIGNAL JAMMER is another piece of tech to never leave unused. Either carry it or use. There is no disadvantage to obfuscating your presence on a planet that is actively trying to kill you. 

  

REMARK: Make them question their own eyes.
