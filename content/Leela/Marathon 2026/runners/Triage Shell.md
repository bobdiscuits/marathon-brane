RUNNERS' GUIDE                                      v.1.001.01.tciv

TRIAGE SHELL  

\=================================================================== 

  

SHELL MODEL: TRIAGE 

ROLE: Field Medic  

TECH: Damage Mitigation 

  

OVERVIEW: This one's for the fixer, the never-diers, the ones who get it done. TRIAGE is no joke. TRIAGE is the one who knows they can't rely on anyone else because everyone else relies on them. To keep em running. To save the day. To get resurrected the "dead." Rocking a TRIAGE SHELL tells your enemies and your crew nothing ends easy. With a healthy suite of medically focused ABILITIES to fix what needs fixing; it's hard to keep a RUNNER outfitted in a TRIAGE SHELL down. 

  

ABILITIES / TRAITS:  

    • MED-DRONE

    • REBOOT+

    • SHAREWARE.EXE

    • BATTERY OVERCHARGE

  

HISTORY: TRIAGE SHELLS were first developed by TRAXUS in support of their Martian mining interests. As SHELL-based workers became the norm, TRAXUS needed a means to keep their workers functioning despite high turnover due to the hazardous conditions. The TRIAGE suite of SHELL ABILITIES started as the answer to those concerns. Developed fully in concert with the UESC and SEKGEN, the TRIAGE SHELL became a target of RUNNER interests shortly before they made their official debut.

  

COMMON SHELL SERIES: The AUX personality matrix that comes pre-wired into most TRIAGE SHELLS was the factory standard when the SHELL line launched. Designed specifically to provide a calming "bedside manner" in traumatic situations, RUNNERS have, like with most of their gear/tech, tweaked the baseline AUX vox patt to maintain its core design while having a bit more flexibility in its aggression to help ensure intents [read: threats, commands, etc.] are properly conveyed when in the heat of run.
