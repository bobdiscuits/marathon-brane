# Riot Barricade

rnnrqterm.cdx.access                        - -:- -.- -.- -:- -.- - 

\=================================================================== 

RUNNERS' GUIDE                                      v.1.001.01.tciv

DESTROYER TACTICAL ABILITY: RIOT BARRICADE 

\=================================================================== 

  

OVERVIEW: Nobody likes a bully. Be a bully. The DESTROYER SHELL's hard light mobile protection platform, or RIOT BARRICADE, is a gauntlet-mounted, personal space defender that allows a RUNNER to step hold ground, defend a retreat, or step into a conflict with confidence and bad intentions intact.  

  

OPERATION: 

   • Activate RIOT BARRICADE 

   • Take damage with no trauma to your shell 

   • Hold or push the line until: 

        • You change tactics, or…

        • RIOT BARRICADE dissipates

             • RIOT BARRICADE's charge dissipates when its power recycles 

             • Incoming damage eats a RIOT BARRICADE's power, threatening

               its durability over time 

  

HISTORY: This variant of a mobile protection platform was first utilized in a security SHELL designed for use by local police forces in cases of extreme social disturbance, hence the name: RIOT BARRICADE. While testing DESTROYER prototypes, the UESC discovered the addition of the BARRICADE to what had previously been a wholly offensively-focused combat suite upped aggressiveness and success rates in live-fire field exercises.
