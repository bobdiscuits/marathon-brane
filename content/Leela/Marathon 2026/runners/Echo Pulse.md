# Echo Pulse

rnnrqterm.cdx.access                        - -:- -.- -.- -:- -.- -

\=================================================================== 

RUNNERS' GUIDE                                      v.1.001.01.tciv

RECON PRIME ABILITY: ECHO PULSE 

\=================================================================== 

  

OVERVIEW: ECHO PULSE's state-of-the-art neural-linked radar bounces a data scraping signal into a local radius around your RECON SHELL, locating and marking anything it reads as an active threat. Its value in reading an engagement gives a RUNNER or crew a strategic advantage. Additionally, if you're in a tight spot, the information ECHO PULSE provides can help subvert enemy intentions. 

  

OPERATION: 

     • Initiate ECHO PULSE 

     • CHECK your surroundings 

     • IDENTIFY marked targets 

     • ENGAGE or ESCAPE 

  

HISTORY: There's a reason RECON SHELLS have been labeled "vigilante kits" by UESC and local investigators. Their array of tracking abilities were put to immediate use by bounty hunters and bagmen once the SHELL's military-grade, UESC-engineered tech hit RUNNER circles. Five incidents of "personal justice" on Earth, two on Mars, and one in a post-orbital space dock near Luna in less than a year made the RECON infamous back in Sol and forced a UESC crackdown on their use.
