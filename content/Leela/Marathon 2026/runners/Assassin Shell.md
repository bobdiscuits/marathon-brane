RUNNERS' GUIDE                                      v.1.001.01.tciv

ASSASSIN SHELL 

\=================================================================== 

  

SHELL MODEL: ASSASSIN 

ROLE: Shadow Agent  

TECH: Invisibility Systems 

  

OVERVIEW: "The unseen blade strikes first." Just one of many tenets of the ASSASSIN. Not that every RUNNER who outfits an ASSASSIN SHELL adheres to the same worldview, but the words remain true—stealth operatives tend to strike first or disappear before their targets can react. Active Camouflage that can be triggered on demand or passively via synth smoke adaptability makes ASSASSINS the spectral death of any ZONE.  

  

ABILITIES / TRAITS:  

     • ACTIVE CAMO 

     • SMOKE SCREEN 

     • SHADOW DIVE 

     • SHROUD 

  

HISTORY: While the UESC has never admitted to developing the underlying tech that makes ASSASSIN SHELLS so advantageous/dangerous during RUNNER operations, it is widely known in RUNNER circles that the stealth technology woven into an ASSASSIN's systems began with UESC Spec Ops prototyping. 

  

COMMON SHELL SERIES: Believed to be the original ASSASSIN SHELL personality series, Void is utilized by ASSASSINS in tribute to a nameless RUNNER involved in early invisibility research. The full truth is locked in SEKGEN/UESC classified files, but many believe that the first ACTIVE CAMO test subjects became casualties of the untested tech, and that early attempts to merge neural transference with invisibility caused for near total loss of memory followed by total loss of self. ASSASSINS believe that the Void series (discovered in old neural coding but never officially released) is patterned after one of those first unknown RUNNERS who lost their minds perfecting the very tech that was their undoing.
