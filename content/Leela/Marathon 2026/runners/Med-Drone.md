# Med-Drone

rnnrqterm.cdx.access                        - -:- -.- -.- -:- -.- - 

\=================================================================== 

RUNNERS' GUIDE                                      v.1.001.01.tciv

TRIAGE TACTICAL ABILITY: MED-DRONE 

\=================================================================== 

  

OVERVIEW: Everyone needs friends. And the TRIAGE SHELL's MED-DRONES are a welcome addition to any party.  Essentially mobile care units, these floating sidekicks sync to your SHELL and/or the SHELL of a crewmate, providing passive healing and shield regen, while also preventing the connected RUNNER from "bleeding out" if downed. 

  

WARNING: They can be targeted and destroyed by your enemies. 

  

OPERATION:

     • Toss a MED-DRONE 

     • For you or a friend

     • ENJOY the soothing benefits 

  

HISTORY: MED-DRONES are companion tech, first engineered by TRAXUS for use with newer model TRIAGE SHELLS. Their mobility allows for the administration of mechanical/medical care to damaged SHELLS working in tight spaces [read: mining operations [read: mining collapse]]. From a RUNNER POV a "tight space" is anywhere you find yourself between live fire, making TRIAGE and MED-DRONES a perfect addition to any crew.
