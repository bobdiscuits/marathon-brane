# Search and Destroy

rnnrqterm.cdx.access                        - -:- -.- -.- -:- -.- - 

\=================================================================== 

RUNNERS' GUIDE                                      v.1.001.01.tciv

DESTROYER PRIME ABILITY: SEARCH AND DESTROY 

\=================================================================== 

  

OVERVIEW: If the DESTROYER SHELL is a weapon of war, then SEARCH AND DESTROY is its shoulder-mounted, impact tracing targeted artillery.  

  

OPERATION: 

   • Trigger SEARCH AND DESTROY 

   • Neural/HUD/Live Round mapping active 

   • Hunt Targets 

   • Find Targets 

   • Aim/Fire/Impact Targets with your chosen firearm 

   • ENJOY 

     • Every third round impacted on Target unleashes a micro-rocket

        salvo tracking directly to impact site 

  

HISTORY: As a dedicated conflict resolution system, the DESTROYER SHELL was engineered from first principles to be an offensive weapon specifically used for surgical strikes against enemy personnel and machinery, in conjunction with larger-scale UESC combat deployments.  

  

A combination of SEARCH AND DESTROY's "too efficient" lethality and reports of mental destabilization in soldiers with prolonged use of its neural/HUD/live round cross-mapping caused the UESC to discontinue use of the DESTROYER SHELL all up. Most RUNNERS assume the off-lining of the DESTROYER was a public relations mandate as cover for various "more terrible" UESC operations.
