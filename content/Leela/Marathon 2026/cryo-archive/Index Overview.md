ROLE: Primary cryostasis storage facility. 

OVERVIEW: INDEX housed, monitored, and maintained Command-level and associated colony personnel. The main functional difference between all primary and secondary CRYO ARCHIVE facilities is their designed operational efficiency. Primary facilities supporting Command operations demanded additional backend and transitioning support as their "patients" required more frequent and, at times, emergency wake and/or sleep cycling.
