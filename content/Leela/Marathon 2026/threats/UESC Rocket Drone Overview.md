AFFIL: Unified Earth Space Council [UESC]

MODEL: Autonomous Aerial Drone [AAD]

CONFIG: Advanced Aerial Bombardier Platform [AABP]

MANUF: UESC Combat Robotics Division, Mars

SERIAL: AABP5012AXXXXX

ROLE[S]: Area overwatch and threat deterrent; Heavy munitions bombardier

  

OVERVIEW: The UESC ROCKET DRONE is an airborne hunter-killer programmed to surveil contested ZONES for unauthorized personnel and engage identified targets via heavy munitions bombardment. The rocket-propelled ordnance unleashed by UESC ROCKET DRONES delivers a destructive blast with a lethal blast radius meant to annihilate their targets.

  

WARNING: UESC ROCKET DRONES unleashed at some point into a RUN, specifically programmed to locate and target RUNNERS. They move REGION-to-REGION and shell RUNNER positions to either eliminate the trespassers or push them out of the ZONE, freeing the UESC to continue their efforts to secure and explore TAU CETI IV free of illegal interference.

  

REMARK: Watch and listen. The skies will become death, but ROCKET DRONES are loud, so stay alert to avoid—or hunt—them.
