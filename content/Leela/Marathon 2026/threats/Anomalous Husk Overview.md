OVERVIEW: Something roams DIRE MARSH at night. Something humanoid but not human—corrupted bits of shells and UESC frames reanimated and angry. Rotten synthetic flesh and frayed circuitry fueled by a volatile rage.

  

The UESC labeled them "HUSKS." It's as good a description as anything else until we can learn more about them beyond the gut reaction of abject fear. Survival in any ZONE is rare, but TAU CETI keeps upping the threat with variables no one could have imagined.

  

Luckily for most RUNNERS, the unknown, no matter how brutally grotesque, is an opportunity—new variables offering new angles toward new paydays. Or new ways to die.

  

REMARK: Don't let them get close. They explode.
