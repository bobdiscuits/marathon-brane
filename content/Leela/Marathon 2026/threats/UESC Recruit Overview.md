THREAT: UESC RECRUIT

\===================================================================

  

TYPE: RECRUIT  

AFFIL: Unified Earth Space Council [UESC] 

MODEL: Autonomous Robotic Soldier [ARS] 

CONFIG: Basic Tactical Combat Platform [BTCP] 

MANUF: UESC Combat Robotics Division, Mars 

SERIAL: BTCP5012BXXXXX 

  

\===================================================================

  

ROLE[S]: First line security and defense, Light infantry, Security specialist 

  

OVERVIEW: The RECRUIT is the base model in the UESC's Autonomous Robotic Soldier initiative. It is designed for general security and light combat duty, with operational programming engineered to function within a linked patrol unit. Its primary role is to identify and engage threats to its assigned area of protection.  

  

Although they are effective as a first layer of defense, their primary value against skilled intruders is as an active early-warning system—once engaged, linked platforms within a RECRUIT's patrol unit move to eliminate any identified threat.   

  

UESC RECRUIT units operate in three variants: 

   • Standard RECRUIT [Pistol] 

   • Breach RECRUIT [Shotgun] 

   • Range RECRUIT [Precision Rifle] 

  

REMARK: Overconfidence in dealing with RECRUIT configs may result in forced ejection.
