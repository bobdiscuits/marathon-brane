\===================================================================

  

TYPE: SCOUT 

AFFIL: Unified Earth Space Council [UESC] 

MODEL: Autonomous Robotic Soldier [ARS] 

CONFIG: Specialized Tactical Combat Platform-Ranged [STCP-R] 

MANUF: UESC Combat Robotics Research Division, Mars 

SERIAL: STCP5033SXXXXX-R 

  

\===================================================================

  

ROLE[S]: Long-range area defense, Precision combat specialist  

  

OVERVIEW: Specialized upgrade from RECRUIT config. UESC SCOUTS are equipped with advanced targeting sensors and optics that allow them to detect and track threats with lethal accuracy. Their primary function is to hold ground and defend distant perimeters. They are not heavily armored and rely on distance and positional maneuvering to balance the offensive and defensive requirements of an engagement.  

  

The SCOUT config's purpose within a patrol unit is not limited to the lethality of its precision firing mechanics. Their ability to identify and engage targets at distance provides any linked units with advanced warning of hostile incursions into protected spaces. 

  

REMARK: Close distance quickly or avoid clean sightlines to limit ranged threat.
