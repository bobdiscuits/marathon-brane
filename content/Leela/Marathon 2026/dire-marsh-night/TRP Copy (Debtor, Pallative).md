//The Interplay Between Credit and Debt//

  

Debt empowers life. Without debt, there is no credit; without credit, humanity does not build its way to the stars—and beyond. The marriage of obligations and trust creates a virtuous spiral of progress, decoupling our species from scarcity while building an engine of motivating growth.

  

//Too Much of a Good Thing//

  

Everyone carries some form of obligation; to be human is to be in debt. However, CyberAcme understands that too much debt can become burdensome. It is our aim to relieve some of that burden so you can get back to helping move humanity forward.

  

If you:

  

+Have accrued personal obligations (medical included) exceeding 9:1debt to income.

  

+Inherited overwhelming K-line ancestral debt

  

+Have Corporate Service Debt exceeding 3:1

  

+Have been penalized by the UESC or a Corporate Entity with Category-P Obligations

  

Then you might be eligible for CyberAcme's industry-leading NextForm Recourse Program.

  

//Your Solution//

  

The NextForm Recourse Program is not a conventional debt consolidation, cryo-accrual wage garnishment, individual obligation securitization, or other partial relief option that limits your ability to live life on your terms. The Program is an end-to-end, total debt clearance schedule that lets you dictate your own relief while earning valuable CyberAcme corporate service credits. By shedding your corporeal form and enrolling in the Program, you remain you—free of UESC-recognized debt.

  

Inquiry—the simple, human act of asking for help—will not affect your credit score. Thanks to our guarantor-state partners in the UESC, CyberAcme is the only Entity able to promise that your baseline credit score will be preserved if you express interest in this Program.

  

By joining our NextForm Recourse Program, you're making the affirmative choice to balance work and life on your terms. Once successfully established via CyberAcme's industry-leading secure decorporealization process, you will be trained, supported, and compensated for your work—either under your preexisting legal identity, or via a new CyberAcme NextForm ID escrow. Accredited via CyberAcme, you'll operate a wide variety of biosynthetic shell systems ("Shells") protected by UESC classifications (Form i1599, Form UESC22, and Form GC\_100).

  

//Smart Decisions Require Informed Consent//

  

Decorporealization can sometimes create feelings of discomfort. Déjà vu is to be expected. Do not worry. We offer industry-leading resources and counseling to help you address and grow comfortable with these feelings; these supportive services are freely available once you conclude your principal obligations and interest.

  

We understand this is a big decision. We've got your back.
