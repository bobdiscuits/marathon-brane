DIRE MARSH [NIGHT] REGION: BIO-RESEARCH

LOCATION: Northeast Marsh

  

INSIGHT: BIO-RESEARCH's tight spaces and subterranean tunnels can quickly become overwhelmed by attacking forces—UESC or RUNNERS. Know your exits and escape routes and understand: Any noise made can quickly draw the attention of rivals in COMPLEX or GREENHOUSE. Darkness can help obscure some activities and movements, but that reality is not one-sided.

  

OVERVIEW: Like the rest of DIRE MARSH, how the work done in these BIO-RESEARCH labs shifted in response to the ANOMALY and contagion in the later days of the colony remains a mystery. RUNNERS continue to scour the REGION in search of info and lost resources they can sell to parties interested in piecing together what happened to inhabitants of NEW CASCADIA.

  

NOTICE: A UESC NETWORK TOWER is located outside BIO-RESEARCH's main research facility.
