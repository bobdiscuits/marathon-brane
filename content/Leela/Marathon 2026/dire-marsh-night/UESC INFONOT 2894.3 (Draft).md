# UESC INFONOT 2894.3 (Draft)

ANALYZING…

FILE TYPE: UESC Pre-Production Draft

PERIOD: Present Day

TOPIC: Notice to All Runners

SUMMARY: A pre-production draft of a UESC propaganda dispatch for subjects (youth) on Earth and in Sol-system colonies.

\=================================================================== 

  

INFONOT Number: 2894.3

  

Effective Time Frame (Valid From/To): UE2894-UE9999(+)

  

Attn: UESC Cat. R\_101 (Resident, Base Privileges); Cat. C\_101 (Citizen, Base+ Privileges); Cat. S\_201, \_301, and \_401; Cat. C\_201.

  

Disregard: None

  

Information/Notice to Follow:

  

Want to Run?

  

"Runners are flash."

  

"Runners don't have debt."

  

"Runners get to do what they want."

  

Think that's true?

  

Think again.

  

Once you've chosen to transition from your birth body to become a non-bodied person, the process is irreversible. Decorporealization is the first step to becoming a "Runner"–and it's one you can't take back.

  

Approved decorporealization within the UESC is a process supervised by permitted specialists. Whether palliative or service-related, your transition is managed to ensure the continuity of rights, privileges, and protections afforded to you by the UESC and the member state of which you are a citizen or resident.

  

If you circumvent this process, you forfeit that protection. You become corporate property. We cannot protect you from the corporation that stores you.

  

Want out?

  

Too late. The biotech companies have got you now, trapped in their servers. They get to tell you when to run, where to run, and for how long. Do you think they'll play fair?

  

They won't. They're playing for keeps.

  

After you sign on, you won't get to say "no." Try, and they'll throw you into a Scheherazade hold, trapping you in a looping frustration for as long as they want. Or they'll put you in corrective cryostasis, removing you from the flow of normal time for decades, even centuries. Maybe they'll bury you under more debt, forcing you to pay the cost of each Shell you Run. How about a nightmare impound, which restricts your mind to a peak-fear experience until your sentence is complete?

  

Still want to be a Runner? Still think oligarch tech-tyrants care about you and your freedom?

  

Think again. The only running you should do is away from them.
