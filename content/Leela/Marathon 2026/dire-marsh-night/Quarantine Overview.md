DIRE MARSH [NIGHT] REGION: QUARANTINE

\===================================================================

  

LOCATION: West Marsh

  

INSIGHT: Even before strange oddities began haunting DIRE MARSH, the derelict QUARANTINE tent at its western edge was a clear indication that the colony's fate may have been darker than imagined. Most RUNNERS prefer to limit their time inside this nightmare relic.

  

OVERVIEW: Bad things happened here. From the necessity for the site itself to the containment pods within and the makeshift memorial just outside, there are clear signs of terrible choices born of terrible circumstances: The extent the contagion played in the colony's ultimate fate, the contagion's ties to the ANOMALY, the various signs of biological and technological corruption found in the evidence RUNNERS have uncovered so far. The mysteries of TAU CETI IV deepen by the run. And that's before we consider the truly alien possibilities we can no longer ignore.

  

NOTICE: A UESC NETWORK TOWER stands near the West Gate security checkpoint just north of QUARANTINE.

  

REMARK: Maybe the voices in the night are those who never left?
