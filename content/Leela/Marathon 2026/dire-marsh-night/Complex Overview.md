DIRE MARSH [NIGHT] REGION: COMPLEX

\===================================================================

  

LOCATION: East Marsh

  

INSIGHT: Newly converted base of operations for UESC Security Forces ANOMALY research.

  

OVERVIEW: COMPLEX's upper interior has been commandeered by UESC Security Forces to serve as a dedicated ANOMALY research and observation facility.

  

The REGION's heightened security measures and the aggression used to defend it from unauthorized access suggests it plays a key role in the UESC's efforts to understand and control access to the ANOMALY, making it a prime target for RUNNER "intervention."

  

The increased activity and direct research into the ANOMALY seem to be tied to strange sightings and unknown dangers across the ZONE.

  

NOTICE: A UESC NETWORK TOWER sits just south of COMPLEX's observation deck.

  

REMARK: Acquire UESC Cert codes to access COMPLEX's upper-level facilities. Fair warning: A UESC Warden guards the anomalous research operations housed in upper COMPLEX.
