DIRE MARSH [NIGHT] REGION: MAINTENANCE

\===================================================================

  

LOCATION: Southwest Marsh

  

INSIGHT: MAINTENANCE is known as a highly-contested ZONE in RUNNER circles. Controlling its second-floor catwalks and rooftops gives a crew an upper hand against attacking rivals as well as clear views to gauge risks coming from or in moving to QUARANTINE, ALGAE PONDS, and INTERSECTION.

  

OVERVIEW: Removed from direct ANOMALY research, the MAINTENANCE facility is left with only the most basic operational power to its lighting and ancillary systems. The neglected state of MAINTENANCE's looming, darkened structure offers RUNNERS plenty of opportunities to scavenge unseen, but it also requires some illumination to loot efficiently in its pitch-black depths.

  

NOTICE: A UESC NETWORK TOWER has been erected to the west of MAINTENANCE's main entrance.

  

REMARK: Use caution with using your flashlight. It will make you a target to unknown threats.
