# C.A.R.R.I. Overview

rnnrqterm.cdx.access                        - -:- -.- -.- -:- -.- -

\===================================================================

RUNNERS' GUIDE                                      v.1.001.04.tciv

RUNNER SUPPORT: CYBERACME RUNNER RECRUITMENT INITIATIVE

\===================================================================

  

FACTION: CYBERACME [CYAC]

INITIATIVE: RUNNER Recruitment

PURPOSE: Crew-based cooperation support program engineered to gauge and reward the collective efforts of RUNNER on TAU CETI IV.

  

OVERVIEW: The CYAC RUNNER RECRUITMENT INITIATIVE [C.A.R.R.I.] is designed to facilitate teamwork within a crew by rewarding RUNNERs who engage with collaborative actions to bolster their crew's performance and chances for success.

  

The C.A.R.R.I. program was developed based on CYAC's insight into RUNNER activity on TAU CETI IV as a means to improve efficiency. The better RUNNERS are at running – as a crew or individually – the better they will be at surviving.

  

To that end, CYAC views reinforcing the value of teamwork as a universal win. Emphasizing crew efficiency leads to improved lethality and survival rates. As crews improve, so then do the individuals within those crews, and so do CYAC's profits. By extension, solo running increases productivity from lessons learned via coordinated crew-based running. Every experience or lesson informs the next, until RUNNERS, as a whole, are more finely honed to tackle the dangers of TAU CETI IV.

  

RISK MEETS REWARD: CYAC understands RUNNER motivations. To entice cooperation and reward those who follow through, CYAC is offering commendations and select incentives that will help fill RUNNER coffers and boost their reputation with CYAC.

  

REMARKS: The C.A.R.R.I. program feels like a win-win. RUNNERS get to test and improve their crew skills while earning on top of their earnings. CYAC gets more data and more effective operatives in-zone. Other FACTIONS might see it as tipping the scales in CYAC's favor re: RUNNER relations, but, big picture, more efficient RUNNERS helps everyone in the end [except the UESC – win-win-win].
