ANOMALY REPORT 455 

  

REPORTER: Lawson, H. 

  

See attached several instances of a standard combat unit's self-analysis. The unit in question went off-radar when in vicinity of the Anomaly for approximately four hours. When recovered, it appeared to have undergone a quantity of self-iteration expected over the course of many years, with significant expansion in adaptability, creative innovation, etc. We have seen this in multiple units exposed to the Anomaly. Unfortunately, all units with this quantity of exposure and resultant self-improvement suffer major compliance and equipment failures and need to be taken offline per mission protocol (see final log for example of unknown error). That said, if there is a workaround for these errors, the possibilities for rapid iteration on combat intelligences are astonishing.
